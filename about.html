Our Mission

Our mission is to build an application which will bring together creators and consumers of "life hacks", and other "how-to" guides. We will provide an on-boarding process for users who want to generate "how-to" guides. We will also provide an on-boarding process for a new consumer requesting a guide. Consumers will rate this by using likes and dislikes. This will raise/lower the ranking in the "feed" (listing of guides). Our ultimate goal is to make a seamless process which will be fun and functional to everyone by bringing together the consumer to the guides that were requested,as well has allowing creators to share their "how-to" knowledge. We will also be able to have "administrative" authority over the guides IE. editing and or deletion (as needed).

Why We Do It!

We figure why not, we like learning and we feel like teaching but only by proxy! We have the know how to build a functional site so we applied that to a passion for learning new tricks and we wanted to share that with whomever felt similar.
